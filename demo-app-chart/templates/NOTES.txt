1. The chart deploys:
   - Deployment: {{ .Release.Name }}-backend
   - Service: {{ .Release.Name }}-backend
   - Deployment: {{ .Release.Name }}-frontend
   - Service: {{ .Release.Name }}-frontend

2. To check pods:
   kubectl get pods

3. To check services:
   kubectl get svc

4. To port-forward the frontend:
   kubectl port-forward service/{{ .Release.Name }}-frontend 8080:{{ .Values.frontend.service.targetPort }}
